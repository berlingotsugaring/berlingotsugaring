What is sugaring?

Sugaring is a hypoallergenic method of hair removal that has been in use since 1900 B.C. Sugaring is often compared to standard waxing. During the process, a sugaring substrate sticks to and essentially removes hair without attaching to the skin. The substrate can be applied at room temperature or heated to a lukewarm temperature, minimizing the risk of burns.

So, what is berlingot?

Berlingots are classic French hard candies known for their stripes, colorfulness, and signature pyramid shape.
